Organisation's History

Helping Hands Animal Rescue South Africa was founded in October 2009 by Athol Fenderick. Per year in South Africa, over 20 000 cases of abandoned, hurt, and unhealthy animals are reported. Helping Hands SA was created to improve the lives of abused and abandoned animals, providing shelter, nutrition, and love. With the help of a committed support committee and generous followers, we raise funds through events such as picnics where you can bring your furry friend and interact with others, making long-lasting friendships while improving the lives of animals. We strongly support no-kill shelters and aim to raise awareness about animal cruelty and neglect.

Mission

Helping Hands Animal Rescue South Africa is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. We aim to improve the welfare of animals and educate communities about responsible pet ownership.

Vision

To provide shelter, nutrition, medical care, and most importantly love to abandoned and abused animals, and to create a compassionate community that values animal welfare.

Success Stories

Bella and Her Puppies

Bella was found abandoned on the streets, weak and malnourished. Through our care and nutrition programs, Bella regained her health and gave birth to a healthy litter of puppies. Today, Bella and her puppies have all found loving homes.

Max Found Love

Max, a stray male dog, was found wandering near a busy road. He was malnourished and frightened. After weeks of rehabilitation and care, Max became a playful and healthy dog and was successfully adopted into a loving family.
