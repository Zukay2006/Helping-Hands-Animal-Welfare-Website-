Welcome to Helping Hands Rescue Animals South Africa

If you are visiting or reading this, you came to the right place!

Per year in South Africa we get over 20,000 cases of abandoned, hurt, and unhealthy animals. Helping Hands Rescue Animal South Africa was founded by Athol Fenderick in October 2009 to help animals in need of shelter, nutrition, and love. Our main purpose is to improve the lives of abused and abandoned animals. Through fundraising events such as picnics and community interactions, we bring awareness to animal cruelty and support no-kill shelters.

The real heroes are the animals that have been hurt but continue to love us unconditionally.

Join the conversation on all social media platforms about changing the lives of our pets and best friends.

Quick Statistic

87% of the animals rescued by Helping Hands Animal Rescue find their “furever” homes within the first 6 months of rescue!
